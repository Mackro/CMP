Insanus the god of chaos and fire. Insanus was one of the most gifted arcane masters among the gods and when war raged Insanus quickly found himself on the front lines destroying everything he saw. Insanus lust for destructive chaos has easily found it's into the minds of many warborn altough only a few are born with his infection. A Warborn infected with Insanus will reign fire on his foes while collapsing all sense of structure meanwhile quickly climbing his way to the top claiming himself as the absolute emperor.
