God of chaos and fire, Insanus was one of the most gifted arcane masters among the gods and when the War of Gods raged, Insanus quickly found himself in the front lines, destroying everything he saw. Insanus' lust for destructive chaos can easily find its way into the minds of many warborn, altough only a few are born with his infection. A Warborn infected with Insanus will reign fire on his foes while collapsing all sense of structure, all the while quickly rising in ranks in his quest of claiming himself as the absolute emperor.
